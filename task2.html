Dr.APJ Abdul Kalam

Missile man and president of India

Dr Abdul Kalam was Indian scientist and politician who played a leading role in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs

Here's a time line of Dr.Apj's life

  • 1931 - born October 15, 1931, Rameswaram, India—died July 27, 2015, Shillong

  • 1958 -Kalam earned a degree in aeronautical engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology and in 1958 joined the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO).

  • 1958 -Rejoining DRDO in 1982, Kalam planned the program that produced a number of successful missiles, which helped earned him the nickname “Missile Man.”

  • 1992 -scientific adviser to the defense minister

  • 1992 -Received Bharat Ratna

  • 1999 -principal scientific adviser (1999–2001) to the government with the rank of cabinet minister.

  • 2002 -He won the 2002 presidential election

  • 2015 -On 27 July 2015 left the world
